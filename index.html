Please be informed of our following dine in protocols:

Your table is reserved up to 1.5 hours from your reservation time.
In our effort to provide you with a prompt service, we kindly ask that your entire group arrives on time. Seats won't be held if late over 15 minutes due to next reservations.
There is a $3 plating fee per head for cakes/dessert should you wish to bring your own.

Please note, changes of time and party size are subject to availability.
2 hours advance cancellation policy.

Party Decorations are subject to management approval.
For quick and efficient payment process, if your group requires the bill to be split; it can be done to maximum of 4 bills.
An automatic 18% gratuity will be applied to party of 5 and above.
