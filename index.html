Our Reservation Policies


Please be informed of our following dine in protocols:

- Your table is reserved up to 1.5 hours from your reservation time.
- In our effort to provide you with a prompt service, we kindly ask that your entire group arrives on time. Seats won't be held if late over 15 minutes due to next reservations.
- There is a $3 plating fee per head for cakes/dessert should you wish to bring your own. - Please note, changes of time and party size are subject to availability. - 2 hours advance cancellation policy. - Party Decorations are subject to management approval.
- For quick and efficient payment process, if your group requires the bill to be split; it can be done to maximum of 4 bills.
- An automatic 18% gratuity will be applied to party of 5 and above.

Who we are

Welcome to Aamara, where we invite you on a culinary journey that transcends time and borders. Nestled in the vibrant heart of Toronto, Aamara is a modern Indian restaurant that pays homage to the rich tapestry of flavors and techniques that have traversed the historical routes of our diverse subcontinent. At Aamara, we take pride in offering a gastronomic experience that transcends expectations, inviting you to savor the essence of India in every bite.

Join us in celebrating the fusion of history, culture, and gastronomy at Aamara - where every meal is a journey through time and taste.
